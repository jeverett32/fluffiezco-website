Party Prep: Questions Answered

Q: How much space do we need for the stuffing station?

A: A minimum of one large dining table or two folding tables is ideal. Our host needs room to lay out the animals, stuffing, and accessories. We can adapt to smaller spaces, but more room means more fun!

Q: How long does a Fluffiez party take?

A: The core stuffing activity usually takes about **45 to 60 minutes**, depending on the number of guests. Our Deluxe and Ultimate packages include a host to ensure the pace is perfect for everyone.

Q: Is the stuffing process safe and mess-free?

A: Yes! We use child-safe, hypoallergenic stuffing and seal the animals using a simple, no-sew velcro or zip closure. Our hosts bring drop cloths and manage the stuffing process to minimize any mess.
